Stephen Jakob is a Fine Art glamour and fashion photographer from the Pacific Northwest.

Hello! I’m so glad you’re here. I’m Stephen, the man behind the camera. I’ve been practicing photography for the better part of a decade, specializing in portrait work. I study and take inspiration from the great painters of old, using similar lighting techniques, such as chiaroscuro, that they used to create their masterpieces.

I mix those skills with modern tools to create the best possible portraits that I can, giving you, the subject, the best possible experience that I can.

I grew up in Salem, having resided here for all my life. The Mid-Willamette Valley is my home, and my refuge. I spend my free time traveling and exploring the forests and mountains of our beautiful valley.

I look forward to the opportunity to capture your perfect portrait! Feel free to message me with any comments or questions, or to book a consult!
